Research & Publications

Pyramid Diffusion for Fine 3D Large Scene Generation

Yuheng Liu, Xinke Li, Xueting Li, Lu Qi, Chongshou Li, Ming-Hsuan Yang
ECCV 2024 (Oral Presentation, top 2%)

This work uses a coarse-to-fine approach with scale-varied diffusion models to generate high-quality outdoor scenes. It successfully generates 3D scenes both unconditionally and conditionally, and it allows for easy fine-tuning across different datasets and generating infinite scenes.

Deep Hierarchical Learning for 3D Semantic Segmentation

Chongshou Li, Yuheng Liu, Xinke Li, Yuning Zhang, Tianrui Li, Junsong Yuan
Under Review, 2024

This work introduces Deep Hierarchical Learning (DHL) for 3D data, enhancing hierarchical embedding learning and class hierarchy construction for effective 3D object and scene segmentation.
