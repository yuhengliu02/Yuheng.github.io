I am a Level-3 (4th year) undergraduate student enrolled in the Computer Science of Southwest Jiaotong University (CN) and University of Leeds (UK). Currently, I’m a visiting student in University of California, Merced (UC Merced), working with Dr. Qi Lu and Dr. Xueting Li, under the supervision of Prof.Ming-Hsuan Yang. Previously, I worked with Dr. Xinke Li, advised by Prof. Chongshou Li in SWJTU.

My research interests mainly focus on diffusion models and 3D point clouds and their applications in computer vision. One of my current research topics is the generation of large-scale outdoor point cloud scenes under the Diffusion Model. I have also previously conducted research on hierarchical label structures in point cloud data for fine-grained semantic segmentation.

I'm always open to discussing ideas and insights with others. If you'd like to chat, please reach out to my email:
